Instructions

  1. Select one of your pieces to see which move it can make. If there are no moves available, try a diffrent piece.
  2. All of the possible moves will be shown in green. Select a green square to make the move.
  3. You can jump over the opponent's pieces and capture them. You can make multiple jumps over the opponent's pieces in one move with the same piece.
  4. By getting a piece to the other side, the current piece will promote to be a king which can move anywhere diagonally and jump over opponent's pieces accordingly
  5. In order to win you have to capture all of your opponent's pieces or block all of their valid moves
  6. Enjoy!
